About

My name is Trung Vu. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Yau Mathematical Sciences Center at Tsinghua University. My mentors are Professor Nicolai Reshetikhin and Professor Hao Wu. I obtained my Ph.D. from University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2025 under Professor Philippe Di Francesco and Professor Rinat Kedem supervision.

Research

I am interested in statistical mechanics and its interaction with other areas such as algebraic combinatorics, probability, discrete complex analysis, and representation theory.
