Welcome to our Reading Group

We are planning to read the textbook Introduction to Finite and Infinite Dimensional Lie (Super)algebras throughout this semester. Our ambitious plan is to finish chapter 1 on finite dimensional Lie Algebra and reaching the basic of Kac-Moody algebra. The reading group will be held weekly and we will take turn present what we learned from the textbook. We will also try to do some exercises from this old class at MIT instructed by Victor Kac
